✦ My Story

From curious
kitchen kid
to real baker.

It all started when I was 11 and watched my grandma pull a perfect loaf of sourdough from the oven. I was hooked. I begged her to teach me, and she did — and I haven't stopped baking since.

Three years later, I make everything from crusty sourdough boules and chewy New York–style bagels, to flaky butter croissants and soft cinnamon rolls. Every single item is made by hand, in my home kitchen, with real ingredients and zero shortcuts.

Ava Bakes is my little dream — a way to share what I love with my community. When you order from me, you're not just getting bread. You're getting 48 hours of love.

"Baking taught me that patience makes everything better — in the kitchen and in life."
